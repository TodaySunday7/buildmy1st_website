About Crush Crush

Crush Crush is a popular dating simulator game developed by Sad Panda Studios. In this charming and humorous game, you'll meet various characters and work to win their affection through conversations, gifts, and activities.

The game combines elements of idle gaming with dating simulation, allowing you to progress even when you're not actively playing. Build relationships, unlock new characters, and enjoy the quirky storylines and character interactions.

With its colorful art style, engaging progression system, and light-hearted humor, Crush Crush offers hours of entertainment for fans of dating sims and casual games alike.

How to Play

  1. Meet Characters: Interact with different characters to start building relationships.
  2. Work Jobs: Earn money by working various jobs to buy gifts and unlock new activities.
  3. Build Stats: Improve your character's attributes like charm, intelligence, and fitness.
  4. Give Gifts: Present characters with gifts to increase their affection towards you.
  5. Go on Dates: Take characters on dates to advance your relationship status.
